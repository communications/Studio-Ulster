What is
Studio
Ulster?

A unique £72m investment in a large-scale Virtual Production studio complex in Belfast, Northern Ireland.

Studio Ulster is a unique c.£72m proposition that integrates virtual Production with traditional film, animation, games and broadcast industry alongside leading edge R&D/VFX capability.
