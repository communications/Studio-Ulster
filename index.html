What is Studio Ulster?

A unique £72m investment in a large-scale Virtual Production studio complex in Belfast, Northern Ireland.

Studio Ulster is a unique £72m, large-scale studio complex in Belfast, Northern Ireland that integrates Virtual Production with traditional film, animation, games and broadcast industry alongside leading edge Research and Development, and Visual FX capability.
